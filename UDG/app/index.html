WHO
WE ARE?

Urban Development Group(UDG)-property investment,development and management focusing on residential and commercial property development in CEE counteries.Business was founded as a number of privately-held companies and investments mostly in residential property in 2008 and structured into group of companies with same investors in 2015.UDG boasts over USD 40 million worth of property developments in its up to five years developments pipeline. The Group has currently 7 ha of land under development for residential and commercial purposes.Operating as an integrated property development provider,UDG business activities cover land identification and aquistion,planning,financing,resource management,project marketing,building design and construction of residential developments commercial offices,industrial buildings and retail centers.
